Don't get caught empty-handed! 08/01/02
Once you have managed to construct your factory and hangar, a host of new options will become available, making life easier for your troops. As well as the vehicles available, you’ll be able to equip your Micro Commandos with 2 different levels of grapple hook for surmounting high objects such as tables and a parachute to avoid rough landings.
Six different weapons are also available to soldiers in the game; 3 for close-quarters combat: the Sword, Mace and Saw; 3 for long range attacks: the Bow, Crossbow and Elephant Gun. There are also 3 different levels of shield to defend against enemy attacks.

What you equip is up to you, just don’t get caught by a pack of rats carrying nothing more than a fly-swatter!!

Team work 05/14/02
When you’re only 10cm tall, even a cute furry kitten becomes a ferocious tiger.
Getting your troops to work together is the only way to overcome all the obstacles in the world of the big. Set up a steady stream of energy, food and materials to expand your base of up to 24 buildings to include a dynamo, factory, warehouse, hangar, dormitory and kitchen. Send out your engineers in their mobile dormitory and kitchen to help out your weary and hungry collectors (or even drop off unconscious troops at the infirmary).
It’s not going to be easy, but a little team work will transform your aliens into a slick, efficient, and deadly band of Micro Commandos.

D.I.Y transport 03/29/02
In Micro Commandos, you can put yourself behind the wheel of all sorts of vehicles: the mobile kitchen (so that your micro commandos have everything at arms’ reach), the mobile dormitory (so they can go camping at will), the motorbike (fast but fragile), the side-car (to transport a passenger), troop transporters (to move around groups more easily)...
Despite these vehicles being made from buttons, clothes pegs, and cotton buds, they are perfectly adapted to the rigours of most situations.
You can be the judge!

Prove your tactical capabilities! 03/22/02
Command the Micro Commandos each with one of the three following jobs : collectors, specialised in harvesting vital resources, soldiers, specialised in fire arms use or engineers, specialised in the construction and repair of vehicles.
Pull together the strength of some and the ingenuity of others to overcome all obstacles (traps, enemy aliens, enemies from the world of the big) and come out victorious.
